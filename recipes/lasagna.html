Lasagna

Delicious home made lasagna!

Ingredients

  • Provologne Cheese
  • String Noodles
  • Pizza Sauce

Steps

  1. Soak the noodles
  2. place the cheese and pizza sauce on noodles
  3. Warm to a melting temperaeture in the oven
  4. enjoy some cheesy noodle lasagna
